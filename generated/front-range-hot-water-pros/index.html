Common Problems We Fix

When Your Water Heater Fails, Everything Stops

No hot water paralyzes your morning — dishes pile up, showers go cold, laundry waits. We've seen it all and we fix it fast.

💧

No Hot Water At All

Complete failure — no heat, no pilot light, nothing. Usually a failed element, thermostat, or gas valve. We diagnose and fix it same day in most cases.

🌡️

Inconsistent Temperature

Water runs hot then cold, or never quite gets hot enough. Sediment buildup is common in Colorado's hard water areas — we flush and restore full performance.

🔧

Leaking or Rusty Water

Pooling water, rust-colored hot water, or strange noises are signs your unit is failing. Act fast — a slow leak can become a flood with no warning.

Common Questions

Quick answers to what Aurora homeowners ask us most.

How quickly can you get to my home?

For most calls before noon, we can schedule same-day service. Emergency visits are also available — call (720) 555-0188 to check availability.

Do you repair all water heater brands?

Yes. We service all major brands including Rheem, A.O. Smith, Bradford White, Navien, Rinnai, and more — both tank and tankless units.

How do I know if I need a repair or replacement?

If your unit is under 10 years old, repair is usually the better option. Over 10–12 years, replacement often makes more financial sense. We'll give you an honest assessment on-site with no pressure either way.

What does a typical repair cost?

Most repairs range from $150–$450 depending on the issue. We provide a firm quote before starting any work so there are no surprises.

Do you offer a warranty on your work?

Yes — all labor is warranted and new unit installations include the full manufacturer's warranty. Ask us for details when you call.
