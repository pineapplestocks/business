FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to what Aurora homeowners ask us most.

How quickly can you get to my home?

For most calls received before noon, we can schedule same-day service. Emergency visits are available — call (720) 555-0188 to check availability.

Do you repair all water heater brands?

Yes. We service all major brands including Rheem, A.O. Smith, Bradford White, Navien, Rinnai, and more — both tank and tankless units.

How do I know if I need a repair or a replacement?

If your unit is under 10 years old, repair is usually the better option. Over 10–12 years, replacement often makes more financial sense. We'll give you an honest assessment on-site.

What does a typical repair cost?

Most repairs range from $150–$450 depending on the issue. We provide a firm quote before starting any work so there are no surprises.

Do you offer any warranty on your work?

Yes — all labor is warranted and new unit installations include the manufacturer's warranty. Ask us for details when you call.
